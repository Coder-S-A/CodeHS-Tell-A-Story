Effects of Global Warming

Some effects of global warming that scientists hav been predicting for a while that are now happening are loss of sea ice, accelerated sea level rise, and longer, more intense heat waves. Some future effects are that temperatures will continue to rise, frost-free season (and growing season) will lengthen, there will be more droughts and heat waves, and many more.
