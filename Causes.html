Biggest cause of Global Warming

A major cause of global warming is greenhouse gases such as water vapor, carbon dioxide, and methane. You might be thinking "we've always had greenhouse gasses; why is global warming important now?" The answer to this question is that over the last century, the burning of fossil fuels like coal and oil has increased the concentration of carbon dioxide. This happens because the coal or oil burning process combines carbon with oxygen in the air to make carbon dioxide.
