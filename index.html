What is global warming?

Global warming is the increasing temperatures in our world. It falls under the umbrella of climate change.

What is the cause of global warming?

A major cause of global warming is greenhouse gases such as water vapor, carbon dioxide, and methane.

What are the effects of global warming?

Some effects of global warming that scientists hav been predicting for a while that are now happening are loss of sea ice, accelerated sea level rise, and longer, more intense heat waves.
